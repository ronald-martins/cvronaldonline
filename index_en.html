About Me

My name is Ronald A. Martins. I am a mechanical engineer with a degree from UNESP and a master's from the University of Brasília, where I conducted research on the numerical simulation of fluid flow in differential pressure flow meters.

Since 2007, I have been working as a patent examiner at the Brazilian National Institute of Industrial Property (INPI), analyzing patents across various technological fields. I have also served as a division head and mentor for new examiners.

I am currently pursuing a degree in Data Science at UNINTER, focusing on AI applications, machine learning, and business intelligence. I also completed a BI Master’s specialization at PUC-Rio. I have hands-on experience with Python, R, MATLAB, and both relational and non-relational databases.

In my free time, I enjoy traveling, scuba diving, snorkeling, and staying connected with nature. I strongly believe in the power of education and technology to drive positive change in society.
